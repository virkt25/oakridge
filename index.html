FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about ABA therapy and our services.

What is ABA Therapy?

Applied Behavior Analysis (ABA) is a scientific approach to understanding behavior and how it is affected by the environment. ABA therapy uses these principles to help children with autism develop essential skills—including communication, social skills, self-care, and academic abilities—while reducing challenging behaviors through positive reinforcement and evidence-based techniques.

Is Oakridge Children Services an approved OAP provider?

Yes, Oakridge Children Services provides services that are eligible for Ontario Autism Program (OAP) funding. We can help you understand the funding options available for your family. Contact us to discuss how OAP funding can support your child's therapy.

What age groups do you serve?

We provide ABA therapy services for children and adolescents. While early intervention often yields the best outcomes, we work with children of various ages to help them develop skills and reach their full potential. Contact us to discuss how we can support your child regardless of their age.

How often will my child need therapy?

The frequency and intensity of therapy is individualized based on your child's needs and goals. During your initial consultation and assessment, our team will recommend a treatment schedule that balances therapeutic benefit with your family's lifestyle. We offer flexible scheduling Monday through Saturday.

What qualifications do your therapists have?

All our ABA therapy programs are supervised by Registered Behaviour Analysts (RBAs). Our therapy team consists of trained Behaviour Therapists who receive ongoing supervision, training, and professional development to ensure the highest quality of care for your child.

How do I get started?

Getting started is easy! Simply contact us to schedule a free consultation. During this meeting, we'll learn about your child and your family's goals, answer your questions, and discuss how our services can help. You can call us at (905) 451-3556, email info@serviceswithoakridge.com, or fill out the contact form below.
